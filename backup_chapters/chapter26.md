But in the aftermath of these storms, strange crystalline formations began to manifest within the eye of many breaches. These were the first Fragments.

II. THE NATURE OF THE FRAGMENTS

Unlike most Spiral anomalies—which destabilized, consumed, or decayed—these Fragments appeared stable. Each Fragment contained condensed Spiral harmonics, locked into solid form. The Fragments were neither purely matter nor energy—they existed in a quantum state between.

Early theorists hypothesized that the Fragments were "self-corrected Spiral knots" — portions of the breach field that, instead of unraveling chaotically, compressed inward, stabilizing themselves in microscopic harmonic balances. "They are both Spiral and anti-Spiral,” one recovered research entry read. “Order born inside collapse.”

III. EXPERIMENTATION AND UNDERSTANDING

The first recovered Fragments were treated with extreme caution. Early researchers observed: When handled improperly, the Fragments could destabilize local energy fields — disrupting machinery, magnetic systems, and atmospheric conditions. However, under controlled Spiral dampening chambers, they could be safely manipulated. Exposure to neural fields (living minds) produced highly unpredictable results — sometimes nothing, sometimes spontaneous resonance, sometimes violent feedback.

Eventually, experimental neural-link studies revealed that when certain individuals made direct contact with calibrated Fragments, a strange synchronization would occur. The Fragment’s resonance would bond with the subject's neurological patterns, embedding itself into the host's body without rejection. The bonded individuals experienced: heightened physical capacity (strength, reflex, perception); accelerated cognitive processing under duress; subconscious interaction with localized Spiral fields — enabling minor manipulations of surrounding energy patterns; increased resilience to Spiral destabilization zones. However, only a small portion of individuals could successfully bond with Fragments. In most cases, neural collapse or Spiral feedback seizures occurred, often leading to death. Those who succeeded became the earliest recorded Fragment-Bound — rare, highly dangerous individuals who could stabilize and direct Spiral flow through their own biological resonance.

IV. THE FRAGMENTS IN THE POST-COLLAPSE ERA

As civilization fractured further, surviving warlords, scientists, and sects began hunting Fragments obsessively. Their rarity made them objects of power, both as weapons and as political leverage. Some were embedded directly into armor or weapons, enhancing Spiral-based technologies. Others were implanted into elite soldiers or ruling elites, granting them limited Spiral dominion. Religious sects emerged who saw Fragment bonding as divine ascension. Entire wars were fought across breach sites simply to secure single clusters of Fragments.
